Service in Hole In One

The Hole In One Resort has many services, including two large golf clubs on large, spacious green spaces. You can book them to play golf with your friends.

The Hole In One Resort has many services, including hotel accommodation, as the resort offers comprehensive hotel accommodation, while providing some packages designated for hotel accommodation.

The Hole In One Resort has many services, including the presence of restaurants and cafes within the resort, where you can eat and order drinks, all within the same resort.

The Hole In One Resort has many services, including a crafts store that contains many tools used for playing golf inside and outside the resort. The store also offers some discounts from time to time.

Packages in Hole In One

The Hole in One Resort offers many packages, including: You can book a full stay for five nights in a special suite, with five rounds on 18 different holes, in addition to one round at the famous (Sea View) course and a free digital training session. (package code: 400)

The Hole in One Resort offers many packages, including: You can book two nights with breakfast and 3 rounds of 18 holes, in addition to one round on the famous (Sea View) course and free cart rental, with a 20% discount at the pro shop. (package code: 401)

The Hole in One Resort offers many packages, including: You can book a one-night stay with breakfast and dinner in any of the resort’s restaurants, and access to courses by paying fees (except for the sea view tour), in addition to renting the club for free. (package code: 402)

Hole in One Resort also allows the creation of customized packages, where you can create tailored packages for events, institutions, and long stays. The resort encourages customers to communicate with the resort's management itself to arrange them.
